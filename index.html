Our Apartments

Apartments Crafted for Comfort

Tucked away in the traditional village of Anopoli, Madares Apartments invites you to experience the authentic side of Crete.
Here, mornings begin with soft light touching the White Mountains and evenings end in complete silence under the stars.
Away from crowded tourist areas, our apartments offer the perfect balance of comfort, nature and genuine Cretan hospitality.
Whether you are looking for a romantic getaway, a hiking base, or simply a place to disconnect from everyday life, this is where calm meets authenticity. Wake up to White Mountains views and the Libyan Sea, breathe the fresh Cretan air and feel at home, surrounded by the sounds of birdsong and the gentle bells of grazing sheep — a truly peaceful Cretan countryside experience.

Anopolis, Sfakia

Your Gateway to Real Crete

Tucked in the mountain village of Anopolis, Madares Apartments sits above the Libyan Sea — just minutes from some of Crete's most remarkable destinations.

More than a stay — an experience.

Explore Anopolis

Hiking & Nature

Legendary gorges, mountain paths and pristine beaches — Aradena, Imbros, Ilingas, Pachnes summit and Glyka Nera all accessible from your doorstep.

Peaceful Stay

Coffee on the balcony, mountain and sea views, complete silence. A sky full of stars at night. The perfect place to disconnect from noise and routine.

Romantic Escape

A quiet village, fresh mountain air and the sense of being far from everything — the ideal setting for couples seeking calm and natural beauty.

Local Traditions

Taste traditional food in local taverns, meet warm Cretan people and experience genuine hospitality alongside seasonal festivals and cultural events.
